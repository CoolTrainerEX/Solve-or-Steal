Introduction

Solve or Steal! is similar to a game of Mahjong. In Mahjong, you must collect a cartain pattern of tiles to win the game. In Solve or Steal!, you must answer the calculus problems in the given time with an answer card. If you fail to solve it in time, your opponents may Steal! the question and answer it. The objective of the game is to collect a certain pattern of answer cards. The first to complete a set wins.

Setup

This game must be played with four players.
